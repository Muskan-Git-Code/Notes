**Automated COD (Cash on Delivery) Notification**

* **Task:** Automate reminders for payment dues at paydate-1 and paydate+15 for ~33k notifications annually, consolidating multiple payments into a single mail per account.

## 💡 Key Tips & Best Practices
- Start with a smile: Greet confidently, engage politely, speak sincerely, communicate clearly and friendly conversation.
- Always ask: *“Am I going in the right direction?”*
- Focus on **coding part** → clarity > speed.
- **STAR Method**: Situation → Task → Action → Result/ Impact.
- Stay Calm, Reiterate the question → think ("Can i take 2-3 min to think?") → pseudocode to outline logic → validate with edge cases → then code.
- If stuck: "I have very limited exposure and not much aware of the internal details, but I think this approach might work.."
- **Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) phases:** Planning, Requirement Analysis, Design, Coding, Testing, Deployment, Maintenance.
- **{Even in meetings or current company}** Instead of saying "I don't know", say "Sure, let me figure it out and get back to you after researching."
